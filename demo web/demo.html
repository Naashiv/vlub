About Our Club

Dedicated to service and leadership development

Who We Are

The Interact Club of Sagarmatha Midtown is a vibrant community of high school students committed to making a difference. As part of the Rotary International family, we combine service with personal growth.

Our Mission

To develop leadership skills through meaningful service projects that benefit our school and community.

Our Vision

A world where young people are empowered to create sustainable change through service and collaboration.
